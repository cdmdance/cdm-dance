About CDM Dance

CDM Dance focuses on making dance approachable, enjoyable, and empowering for every student. Lessons are customized to your goals, pace, and preferred learning style.

From first-time dancers to those returning after a break, you’ll learn clear foundations, simple patterns, and partner connection skills that build real confidence.

Serving Tampa and surrounding areas, CDM Dance offers flexible scheduling and a welcoming, judgment-free atmosphere in every lesson.
